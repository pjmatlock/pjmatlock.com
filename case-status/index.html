Case Status: All Criminal Charges Dismissed (March 20, 2024)

United States v. Constantinescu et al., S.D. Tex. — Judge Andrew S. Hanen

On March 20, 2024, the U.S. District Court for the Southern District of Texas dismissed all criminal charges in the matter referenced above. The government has appealed to the U.S. Court of Appeals for the Fifth Circuit. A related SEC civil action has been stayed pending the appeal.
